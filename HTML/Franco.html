Swiss Franc - CHF - Fr

In the back of a new constitution, 1850 brought a formal agreement (the Federal Coining Act) establishing the Swiss franc as Switzerland's sole currency; following the introduction of the French franc. In 1865 the Latin American Monetary Union was established, according to which Switzerland (together with France, Italy and Belgium) agreed to tie the franc to a standard value of gold or silver.
