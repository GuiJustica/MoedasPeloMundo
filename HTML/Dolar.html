American dollar - USD - $

The US dollar is one of the most important currencies in the world. Its hegemony is undeniable, since it is used in the adoption of international reserves by Central Banks of many countries and as a reference in any business at a global level. This hegemony is relatively recent, only occurred after the end of World War II. The dollar was devised from the need to create a currency that was able to finance the United States War of Independence in 1776, and nurture the new nation. In 1786 the Continental Congress of the already independent 13 colonies approved the dollar as national currency. The name comes from the word thaler, known silver coin that circulated in Europe during Century XV.
