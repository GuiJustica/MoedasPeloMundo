Canadian Dollar - CAD - C$

The Canadian dollar is an official currency of Canada, has the symbol $ or C$, to differentiate the other dollars. The issuance is the Bank of Canada. It is also commonly known as "buck". As of January 1, 1858, Canada adopted as its currency the Canadian dollar, which was divisible into one hundred cents, equivalent to the United States dollar. That same year, the first local coins were minted. They were then abandoned as Canadian pounds set in 1841 by Britain, a sovereign nation.
