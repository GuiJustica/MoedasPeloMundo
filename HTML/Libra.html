Pound sterling - GBP - £

The Pound Sterling, the official currency of the United Kingdom (England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland). The pound sterling is also the official currency of the British overseas territories (Cayman Islands, Falkland Islands, Bermuda, Anguilla, Gibraltar, British Virgin Islands, Pitcairn Islands, South Georgia, St. Helena and Monte Serrat). It has been present in the economy since the year 928.King Athelstan, the first King of England, was the one who instituted the pound as the official currency of the country and proceeded to produce it with money from the crown.
